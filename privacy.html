{/* Introduction */}
Welcome to inkLedger ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and website (collectively, the "Service"). inkLedger is a booking and payment management platform designed for tattoo artists and their clients. By using our Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.
{/* Information We Collect */}
2.1 Information You Provide We collect information you provide directly to us: Account information: name, email address, phone number, artist/business name Profile information: bio, location, social media links, profile picture Booking information: appointment dates, times, tattoo descriptions, placement, size preferences Client information: client names, contact details, booking history, notes Payment information: processed securely through Stripe (we do not store full card details) Reference photos and design images uploaded for bookings Communications: messages, feedback, and support inquiries 2.2 Information Collected Automatically When you use our Service, we automatically collect: Device information: device type, operating system, unique device identifiers Log information: access times, pages viewed, app features used Location information: general location based on IP address
{/* How We Use Your Information */}
We use the information we collect to: Provide, maintain, and improve our Service Process bookings and facilitate payments between artists and clients Send transactional notifications (booking confirmations, design approvals, reminders) via SMS and email Generate financial reports and VAT/BTW calculations for artists Provide customer support and respond to inquiries Send service-related announcements and updates Detect, prevent, and address technical issues and fraud Comply with legal obligations
{/* Payment Processing */}
We use Stripe as our payment processor for handling deposits and payments. When you make a payment: Payment card details are collected and processed directly by Stripe We do not store complete credit card numbers on our servers Stripe's privacy policy applies to payment data: stripe.com/privacy We support iDEAL, credit/debit cards, and Apple Pay For artists: deposits are transferred directly to your connected Stripe account. A platform fee (8%) is added on top of the deposit and paid by the client to cover transaction costs and service fees.
{/* Data Sharing */}
We may share your information in the following circumstances: 5.1 Between Artists and Clients Client contact information is shared with artists for booking purposes Artist business information is visible to clients during booking 5.2 Service Providers We work with third-party services to operate our platform: Stripe: Payment processing Bird (MessageBird): SMS notifications Supabase: Database and authentication Expo: Mobile app infrastructure 5.3 Legal Requirements We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or government request, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of inkLedger, our users, or others.
{/* Data Retention */}
We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Financial records are retained for 7 years to comply with tax regulations. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.
{/* Data Security */}
We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information: Encryption of data in transit (HTTPS/TLS) Secure database storage with access controls Regular security assessments Limited employee access to personal data However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.
